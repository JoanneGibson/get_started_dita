Method for DNA extraction

To extract the DNA:

  1. Suspend 50 mg of tissue in 2 mL of SE buffer (75 mM NaCl, 25 mM EDTA pH 8.0).
  2. Pass the suspension through a 19 gauge (G) syringe.
  3. Add Proteinase K to a final concentration of 200 ug.
  4. Add 1% SDS. Mix. Incubate overnight at 37C.
  5. Add 2 M NaCl. Vortex for 20 seconds. Centrifuge at 1000 x g/ 20 minutes/ 25 C.
  6. Transfer supernatant to a fresh tube and add 2 volumes of 100% (v/v) cold ethanol.
